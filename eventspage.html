Events

Our events are a great opportunity to get involved and support our mission. Whether you want to volunteer your time, make a donation, or simply learn more about the work that we do, there is an event for everyone. Our events are a chance to come together with like-minded individuals and work towards a common goal: creating a better world for all.

Providing aid to the underprivileged 26-dec-2022.

Description

On Monday, Tula Arun Kumar, the chairman of the Tula Subhash Welfare Organization, went to the Bazar Hatnoor Mandal village of Gokonda to identify the lonely and poor elderly residents there and to demonstrate humanity by giving them supplies. He has been participating in numerous volunteer projects through the Tula Subhash Welfare Society for the past few years, and the village's youth invited him to visit at their request. Vice MPP Poreddy Srinivas, who spoke at the event, praised Tula Arun Kumar for all of the charitable work he is doing on behalf of the Tula Subhash Welfare Society. Among those who took part in this programme were Sarpanch Rukmini Jangu, ITDA Director Mesram Bhumanna, Bakri Ramesh, Ganesh, and young people from the village

Distribution of blankets under the auspices of Tula Subhash Welfare Society.. 12-jan-2023

Description

For the benefit of the local SC colony of Sonala, Tula Subhash Welfare Organization launched a blanket distribution campaign. The main guest at the programme to distribute blankets was Tula Ajay Kumar. The women were appreciated by the elderly for setting up the blanket distribution initiative to keep them warm. Amrit Rao Patil, director of the Agricultural Market Committee, Joshna Santosh, Chetlapelli Sudhir Kumar, local youth Bhimrao Patil, B. Ravinder, and Laxman, as well as other women and senior citizens, took part in this activity.

Tula Arun visited the victim's family 26-dec-2022

Description

After learning of Gevande Rama's passing in the Bazar Hatnoor mandal's Gokonda hamlet, Tula Arun Kumar, a State Advisory Member of the International Human Rights Organization Committee, paid them a visit on Monday. There were questions about what happened and why. Subsequently, he made a request for the doctors at the primary health centre near the mandal centre where the incident had occurred. Furthermore, the health center's amenities and issues were noted. According to him, the delivery is being delayed even though there are legs in the medical facility. Additionally, he pledged to bring this occurrence to the commission's notice and see that justice was served. He attended this programme alongside Vice MPP Poreddy Srinivas and Mesram Bhumanna.

Request for road repair 26 Dec 2022

Description

The main road going to Gokonda village has been swept away due to heavy rains, making it perilous, and Tula Arun Kumar told the R&B Department via Twitter that this is the cause of the students and locals' extreme troubles. Also, he declared that he would make a written request. Participants in this session included Sarpanch Rukmini Jangu, Itda Director Mesram Bhumanna, Bakri Ramesh, Ganesh, village youth, and others.

Independence Day celebrations by Tula Arun Kumar with kids 15 Aug 2022

Description

In celebration of the 75th Independence Day, school bags were distributed to about 150 students of Darampuri, Sarpanch Guda and Tukan Pally villages of Bazar Hatnoor Mandal under the auspices of Tula Subhash Welfare Society with the support of Aham Prana Society.On this occasion, Tula Arun Kumar said that with the aim of increasing the educational standards of the students in the tribal areas and supporting their educational development, various materials are being provided to the students and school bags have been distributed as a part of it.Similarly, Vice MPP Poreddy Srinivas said that for the past 15 years under the auspices of Tula Subhash Welfare Society, they have provided career guidance to students as well as smart TVs and other equipment to various schools, and said that their service during the Corona period is immeasurable. Darampuri Sarpanch Arka Kavitha Yadav, Gram Patel Nandev, Panchayat Secretary Srinivas, Degama Lo Chairman Jangu, youths Srikanth, Prashant, Naresh, Raju, Akhil, Rakesh and others participated in this program.

Distributing blankets and sweaters to tribal members with the assistance of the Ahamprana Foundation 24 Dec 2021

Description

Due to the increasing cold in Adilabad district day by day, the popular voluntary organization Aham Foundation, with the help of Tula Subhash Welfare Society Chairman Tula Arun Kumar, is helping the people of his own mandal in the neighboring villages of Vajjar Gram Panchayat, a remote village panchayat of Bhot mandal where the tribal people live. Blankets were distributed to more than 150 elderly people and sweaters to small children.Tula Subhash Welfare Society Chairman Tula Arun Kumar Garu said that I have provided such assistance to the people living in tribal villages and will organize more social service programs in the future. ITDA Director Mesram Bhumanna, Aham Prana Foundation management members Praveen, Nani, Raju, Sarpanchs, Upasarpanchs of the respective villages were present in this program. Leaders and Allamkonda Potanna, Bore Ravi, Pratap Singh and others participated.
