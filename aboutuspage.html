ABOUT US

Tula Subash Welfare Society is a non-governmental organization (NGO) with a vision to improve the quality of life for underprivileged communities and promote sustainable development. The organization works to empower individuals and communities through education, health, and economic programs. The goal of Tula Subash Welfare Society is to create a society where everyone has equal opportunities to lead fulfilling lives and reach their full potential. The NGO also seeks to raise awareness about important social issues and advocate for the rights of marginalized groups. With its dedicated team of volunteers and supporters, Tula Subash Welfare Society is committed to making a positive impact in the communities it serves.

OUR APPROACH

We reach out to children and make them interact with young people from universities and teach them in a friendly way so as to provide them with a family environment for learning, make them understand our culture and hardships they need to overcome in the journey of their life.Our main motives are making children aware of the current scenario in which the world exists and train them to overcome the taboos of society. We also make them aware of the social issues they are in and how to make it a betterplace for them to survive and grow.A well defined smile is what we care for and try to achieve with our constant approach.
